OVERVIEW OF RESOLUTIONS AND DECLARATIONS

What is a Resolution

UN resolutions are official documents adopted by the United Nations. They serve to address important issues, represent political positions and find solutions to global problems. These resolutions are discussed in the various bodies of the United Nations and adopted by the member states. They may contain policy measures, recommendations or decisions adopted by member states to address specific issues, be it peace and security, human rights, environmental issues or other global issues.
